Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay, booking process, and more. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us!

Q: What time is check-in and check-out?

A: Depending on your preffered stay.

Q: Do you allow late check-out?

A: Yes, but additional charges may apply.

Q: Is parking available at the hotel?

A: Yes, we offer free on-site parking for all the guests

Q: How can I book a room?

A: You can book directly on our website, call us or message us of Facebook Page

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online transfers.

Q: Can I cancel or modify my booking?

A: Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before check-in for free.

Q: Do you have an in-house meals to order?

A: Yes, but we only have breakfast meals but you can order it anytime depending on availability.

Q: Do you offer room service?

A: Yes, room service is available from 7:00am to 10:00pm

Q: Are there any tourist attractions nearby?

A: Yes, we are close to Fort Ramon Magsaysay Military Camp Base, Minalungao National Park, and Pahingahan Dam

Q: Do you allow pets?

A: YES! Your furbabies are welcome in the hotel.
